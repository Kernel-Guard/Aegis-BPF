About Daemon Enforcement

The AegisBPF daemon runs on each node as a DaemonSet and enforces the merged policy ConfigMap.

Enforcement requires BPF LSM support (kernel 5.7+), BTF, and ringbuf. Check daemon logs with kubectl logs -n aegisbpf-system -l app.kubernetes.io/name=aegisbpf

Per-node posture reporting will be available in a future release.
